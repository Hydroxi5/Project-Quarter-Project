The 2nd Dimension: Space

The Aspect

Space is a primarily practicle aspect. The wielder can expand and contract space at will, as well as connecting two seperate points in space for instantanious travel.

Government

The Second Dimension has many different nations, each ruled in different ways.

Technology

Mid-ranged technology is present, allowing for the building of large structures of artificial material such as concrete. Standard entertainment is available to most.

Society

The core planet consists of two primary species, Humans and Draconis. As the weaker group, Humans are looked down on and often used as servents for high-ranking Draconis. Otherwise, the dimension is simmilar in society to the 3rd Dimension.

Notable Creations

Rumor has it, the Draconis are working on a new type of weapon that warps space to create devistating damage. The likelyhood of the rumors being true is low, but still a possibility...
