The 3rd Dimension: Matter

The Aspect

Matter is an aspect that allows the weilder to manipulate matter at it's smallest states. A weilder can move matter freely, change it's state, or even change the element itself by manipulating the protons, neutrons, and electrons of an atom or set of atoms.

Government

The 3rd Dimension is populated by many different nations, each ruled in different ways.

Technology

Mid-ranged Technology exists. Advancements in entertainment, such as 'Virtual Reality' have created a type of escapism among several groups of people known as 'Gamers'.

Society

Simmilar to the 2nd dimension, the citizens of several nations have a surpiriority complex. Unlike in the Second Dimension, they are unable to back it up with anything other than 'because we are'.

Notable Creations

The Nation known as 'America' was the first to develop a weapon that utelized the energy released by the fusion of matter. It was named the 'Atomic Bomb'
