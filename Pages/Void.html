The 5th Dimension: Void

The Aspect

Void allows destruction of matter and artificially linked space with the power of 'nothingness'.

Government

The Void-specialized royal guard rules over the remainder of the planet.

Technology

Galactic travel was possible, but not often used. Before an expidition could be launched to colonize a new world, Void was destroyed in an event known to them as 'Heaven's fall'

Society

The Nation fell into poverty after Heavan's Fall, and are extremely distainfull of their new ruler. One group is planning a rebelion, but doesn't have enough power to act.

Notable Creations

None, as of now.
