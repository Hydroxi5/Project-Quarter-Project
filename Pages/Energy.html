The 4th Dimension: Energy

The Aspect

Energy allows the altering of the way energy is transfered, absorbed, and reflected. Energy can be used to reinforce the body, enhance the mind, or move objects at will by altering the kinetic and potential energies of the object.

Government

The 4th Dimension has a unified world under a Rebublic.

Technology

High-Level technology exists, allowing full Solar-System travel with reasonable time. Holographic Technology allows for advanced emulation of environments, and full AI is right around the corner.

Society

Pretty normal. No racial problems, no tyranical government. In total, probably the most normal dimension in terms of public interaction. Supprisingly, this is the only dimension to have common use of an aspect, resulting in the creaton of several schools to teach students about their powers.

Notable Creations

Aster Acadamy, a school with its own city built into a mountain.
